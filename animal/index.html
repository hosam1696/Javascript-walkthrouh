Cardinal

    Fun Facts
  • when a female cardinal sings from the nest it usually means she's telling the male she needs more food
  • The Northern Cardinal is the state bird of seven states Illinois, Indiana, Kentucky, North Carolina, Ohio, Virginia and West Virginia
  • The male Northern Cardinal is perhaps responsible for getting more people to open up a field guide than any other bird

Fox

    Fun Facts
  • A group of foxes is called a skulk or leash
  • Foxes have whiskers on their legs and face, which help them to navigate
  • Grey foxes can retract their claws like cats do

ClownFish

    Fun Facts
  • Clownfish live up to 10 years in the wild but on average up to 6 - 8 years
  • The largest clownfish can reach 18 cm (7.1 in), and the smallest 10 cm (3.9 in)
  • Clownfish species include yellow, orange, reddish and blackish varieties with most covered in patches or bars of white color
